About Me

From a very young age I was exposed to a great deal of travel. Having grown up in Santa Barbara it was important to my parents that I see how fortunate we were, thus by the age of eighteen I had been in and out of somewhere around seventy different countries. Having also learned Russian at a very young age I spent time in Yalta during the early nineties translating at the latter stages of the Cold War. Shortly after High School I spent six months in Ammand, Jordan on an archeological dig before attending Oxford University for a year to study Literature. After finishing up College in the states I relocated to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting. It did not take me long to realize that I was not going to take Hollywood by storm so I began working in Private Equity and Investment Banking as an Analyst for a small Broker/Dealer. After the Recession in 2009 the financial landscape changed with regards to my employers so I relocated to Santa Barbara and have spent the last ten years managing Restaurants/Nightclubs. The hospitality industry plummeted during Covid-19 so I am now studying web-development through a coding bootcamp through UC Davis.
